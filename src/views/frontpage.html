About Us

A Unique Venue In The Heart of London … St Mary’s is a unique London venue. Excellently located just north of Oxford Street, St Mary’s is a stunning Grade 1 listed building that underwent a £3.7 million renovation in 2002. The extensive work not only restored the building to its original Georgian splendour but also significantly expanded and modernised its facilities. In 2014 the sound system, audio visual system and lighting were all also upgraded. The venue boasts a prestigious, light and spacious main room and a large lower floor. These two main areas combined provide over 780 square metres of open space. With superb and highly flexible facilities, St Mary’s is an excellent choice for any event: conferences, product launches, exhibitions, dinners, drinks receptions, fashion shows, concerts, examinations. Our friendly and efficient staff work with you at every stage, dedicated to making your event a success. This, combined with competitive and affordable rates makes St Mary’s one of the most sought-after venues in central London. Previous clients of St Mary’s include the BBC, BT, Royal College of Physicians, Cancer Research UK, London Fashion Week, Matalan, and the Toy Retailers Association. St Mary’s is situated on Wyndham Place, between York Street and Crawford Street, W1H 1PQ, a short walking distance from Baker Street, Marylebone and Edgware Road tube stations.
